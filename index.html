iOS Developer at Buy Me a Coffee

Savespace: Photo Cleaner

I built this SwiftUI photo management app from scratch to help users free up storage and organise their photo library. I used the Vision framework for duplicate detection, CoreGraphics for blur analysis, and created a custom disk-backed persistence layer with FeaturePrintStore. It includes a swipe-based organisation flow and StoreKit paywalls/subscriptions, delivered with a privacy-first approach.
